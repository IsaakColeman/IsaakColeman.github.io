    My Computer

    I picked up playing videogames early because of my dad. Growing up playing Half-Life and old Counter-Strike brought an appreciation for the geneius required to approximate real world lighting and sound in a convincing but computationally sensitive way. This also fostered an excitement around the evolution of hardware and growing "pro-sumer" market.

    I started doing online IT and hardware troubleshooting as a hobby and during my graduation year of high school finally finished my first complete computer build. It had been a dream of mine for a while and it was the first project I researched, planned, financed, and executed all on my own.

    The current specs are:
    CPU: i7-7700k
    GPU: GTX 1080ti
    RAM: 16Gb @ 3200MHz
    I swear the lights make it faster :3
